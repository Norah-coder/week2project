AKAN NAMES

Introduction

The Akan is small group in Ghana.Akan names are derived from Ghananian culture.Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first names as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Therefore,most people knoe their birth dates but they dont know the specific day. Fortunately there are ways to know the specific day using the dates.

Male name

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female name

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
