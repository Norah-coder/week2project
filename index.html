AKAN NAMES

Introduction

The Akan is small group in Ghana.Akan names are derived from Ghananian culture.Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first names as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Therefore,most people know their birth dates but they dont know the specific day. Fortunately there are ways to know the specific day using the dates.Fill in your name, date of birth in the form below so as to now your Akan Name:

Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame
